BroadSound continues to amaze audiences with its world class vocalists and musicians, earning the reputation of being one of the top bands in the country. The bands chemistry is second to none and their energy is absorbed by each audience creating life long memories.

Members of BroadSound have shared the stage with artists such as Ray Charles, Al Jarreau, Aretha Franklin, Patti LeBelle, David Foster, Melissa Manchester, George Duke, Stephanie Mills, Eric Roberson, Hugh Masekela, Common, Kirk Whalum and Lalah Hathaway. BroadSound is simply amazing!
