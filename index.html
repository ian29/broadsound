BroadSound continues to amaze audiences with its world class vocalists and musicians, earning the reputation of being one of the top bands in the country. The bands chemistry is second to none and their energy is absorbed by each audience creating life long memories.

Members of BroadSound have shared the stage with artists such as Ray Charles, Al Jarreau, Aretha Franklin, Patti LeBelle, David Foster, Melissa Manchester, George Duke, Stephanie Mills, Eric Roberson, Hugh Masekela, Common, Kirk Whalum and Lalah Hathaway. BroadSound is simply amazing!

FAQ

  • Q: Our first dance is not on your list. Can you learn it?
  • A: YES. we will learn your first dance. Just give us at least three weeks notice for any special requests.
  • Q: Will you be the MC for the evening or do I have to bring in someone.
  • A: We will be your MC and do all the announcing including the wedding party, toasts or anything you have planned. We will also work with your coordinator and timeline to help keep the flow of your special day moving.
  • Q: I was at a wedding and the band was too loud. We could not hear each other speak during dinner. Is this normal for bands?
  • A: NO! We play to the volume appropriate for that room and situation. You can hear clearly during dinner and we work with your coordinator during the evening to make sure the volume is appropriate.
  • Q: Can you provide music for our cocktail reception?
  • A: YES. There are a number of options available to you. Please contact us and we will be happy to assist in finding the best option that fits your needs.
  • Q: Should we check the songs from your song list we would enjoy hearing during the recetion?
  • A: ABSOLUTLY! We would like your feedback regarding songs you prefer and will customize our set list based on your suggestions. We also continuously read the crowd and adjust the music accordingly with additional songs from out song list to keep guests dancing.
  • Q: Do you guarantee the same musicians and singers will be the same at every event?
  • A: UNFORTUNATELY NOT — Sometimes members go on vacation, attend weddings, become ill or an emergency may pop up. In theses cases we do guarantee the same quality of service. If need be we will provide a sub who has worked with us and is familiar with our material.
  • Q: Do you provide a microphone for toasts?
  • A: YES. We will provide a wireless microphone so anyone giving a toast or speech may do so from any location.
  • Q: Do you enter the crowd, go to tables and entice guests to dance?
  • A: SOMETIMES — This is not for everyone. We will certainly interact with the guests when appropriate but will not force someone to dance or make anyone feel uncomfortable at any time.
  • Q: Do you require a stage for the band?
  • A: NO. Each venue is different and sometimes to save space we will go without but many times they are appropriate. Our minimum stage requirements are 24X12. This should be discussed along with you planner.
  • Q: Our First Dance is pretty long. Is there any way to shorten it?
  • A: YES: We do this all the time. Depending on the song we typically make it 2-2 1/2 minutes if requested. We will work with you to make sure you are comfortable.
